**Azure Sales & Pre-Sales Toolbox**

A curated collection of essential tools and resources to support Azure sales, pre-sales, architecture design, and real-world implementations.

---

<u>**Pricing & Cost**</u>

**» Azure Pricing Calculator**  
https://azure.microsoft.com/pricing/calculator/  
Use this to quickly estimate costs during customer conversations.

**» Azure TCO Calculator**  
https://azure.microsoft.com/pricing/tco/calculator/  
Best for comparing on-premises environments with Azure.

**» Azure Savings Plans**  
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cost-management-billing/savings-plan/  
Use this when discussing long-term cost optimization.

**» Cost Management Best Practices**  
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/cost-management-billing/costs/cost-mgt-best-practices  
Understand how to actively manage and reduce Azure spend.

**» Azure Retail Prices API**  
https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/rest/api/cost-management-billing/retail-prices/azure-retail-prices  
Use this for real pricing data and advanced analysis.
