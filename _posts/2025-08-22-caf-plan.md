## Intro

Most Cloud Adoption Framework guides start by building platforms. This one starts with **people and a plan**. 

If you‚Äôre an MSP or system integrator with on-prem customers or your own data center, use this **hybrid-first path** to turn strategy into action fast:<br>
‚ñ∏ Confirm outcomes<br>
‚ñ∏ Assign roles using **RACI**<br>
(Responsible, Accountable, Consulted, Informed)<br>
‚ñ∏ Snapshot the **digital estate**<br>
‚ñ∏ Choose the **‚Äú8 Rs‚Äù** per workload<br>
(**Retire, Retain, Rehost, Replatform, Refactor, Rearchitect, Rebuild, Replace**)<br>
‚ñ∏ And **sequence the first migration wave** before touching landing zones<br>

Along the way you‚Äôll get ready-to-use assets:<br>
‚ñ∏ A printable Workshop Playmat<br>
‚ñ∏ An **Extended Security Updates and Support cheat sheet**<br>
‚ñ∏ A **VMware exit decision tree**<br>
‚ñ∏ And a **PowerShell inventory script**

The result is a **living adoption backlog** that respects real-world constraints and actually ships value.

## Quick checklist (tick before touching platforms)<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Accountability set:** <br>
Each Wave 1 workload has a named **A** (Accountable) and an **R** (Responsible)<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Capacity secured:** <br>
Adoption & Platform teams have bandwidth and **time-boxed** commitments<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Security in the loop:** <br>
Security/Compliance are **C** (Consulted) with a defined review **SLA** (e.g., 3 business days)<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Cadence agreed:** <br>
Reporting rhythm set (weekly/bi-weekly) with a short, consistent template<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Escalation path:** <br>
Who unblocks decisions within **24h‚Äì48h** is documented<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Kick-off booked (60 min):** <br>
Outcomes/KPIs (10) ‚Üí Wave 1 candidates (15) ‚Üí Draft RACI (15) ‚Üí Next steps (20)<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Published & visible:** <br>
RACI stored in a shared location; owners linked on backlog items<br>
‚ñ∏ [ ] **Next 7‚Äì10 days committed:** <br>
3‚Äì5 tasks assigned with owners & due dates (e.g., estate snapshot, 8Rs workshop, security review)<br>
